# **App Name**: Biotune

## Core Features:

- Immersive Introduction Flow: An animated opening sequence featuring a soft, changing background, a gently floating mascot, and word-by-word text animations leading to an interactive 'CREATE MY SOUND' prompt.
- Generative Biometric Music Tool: Interprets live audio input (microphone for pads/ambience) and device motion (accelerometer for drum triggers) to generate and influence musical elements dynamically. This tool ensures all outputs are inherently musical while incorporating user's real-time interaction.
- Expressive Mascot System: An animated 3D or 2D character that idly floats, naturally blinks, performs subtle talking animations alongside text, and reacts visibly to the music's tempo, intensity, and user interactions.
- Intuitive Melody Creator: A simple visual interface where users can tap to place notes on a piano roll, receiving immediate audible feedback to construct basic melodic patterns.
- Reactive Rhythm Pad: A visual drum pad interface that allows users to manually tap out rhythms or have them triggered dynamically by detected device movements.
- Configurable Music Loop Engine: Enables users to easily define the structure of their music by setting parameters for note and chord counts, and specifying the length of musical loops.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background Color: A dark, desaturated magenta-purple (`#361F30`). This hue evokes a dreamlike quality and allows for vivid elements to pop in activation moments.
- Primary Color: A vibrant, deep magenta (`#E526AB`). This color represents the energetic and magical core of the user's presence, providing a warm, inviting visual anchor against the darker background.
- Accent Color: A bright, enchanting violet (`#EE80FF`). This accent, analogous to the primary, is used for highlights and interactive elements, amplifying the magical and intuitive feel of the application.
- Headline and body text font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for its blend of modernity, legibility, and a touch of warmth, supporting the friendly and engaging experience.
- Icons should be minimal, fluid, and reactive, designed to complement the animated mascot and contribute to the app's overall organic and magical aesthetic.
- Minimal, uncluttered, and highly focused layouts. Key interactive elements are centrally placed with ample negative space to emphasize dynamic content like the mascot and visual energy animations.
- Dynamic and ever-responding animations are integral to the experience. From soft background motions and the gently floating mascot to word-by-word text reveals and reactions to sound intensity, every element should feel alive.
